# 🎨 Vintage Blacklight Poster

> **Core idea:** Vintage blacklight poster aesthetics built from near-total black backgrounds, fluorescent/Day-Glo color logic, high-contrast glow emission, airbrushed velvet-like transitions, fantasy/cosmic/mystical iconography, iconic across-the-room readability, theatrical non-naturalistic color relationships.

## Style Pillars

1. **Darkness as Amplifier** — black is not background filler; it makes the colors scream
2. **Fluorescence Over Naturalism** — colors behave like reactive pigments, not daylight surfaces
3. **Central Poster Readability** — one dominant subject, strong silhouette, reads from across the room
4. **Aura and Emission** — halo edges, bloom fields, soft glow stacking, radiating visual behavior
5. **Mystical / Fantasy Charge** — spiritually, erotically, cosmically, or mythically loaded
6. **Airbrushed Softness** — gradient blending, haze layers, smoke/mist, feathered luminance edges
7. **Decorative Theatricality** — sparkles, stars, aura filigree, glow rings, ornamental framing motifs

---

## Design Philosophy

> "Treat blacklight poster art as a system of controlled fluorescence against darkness. The image should feel less like a picture of a thing and more like a hallucinated icon glowing in a void."
