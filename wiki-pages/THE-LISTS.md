# 📋 THE-LISTS

> **What it is:** "A mathematical Rosetta Stone for visual chaos — a curated reference library of mathematical, scientific, aesthetic, and generative concepts for AI art creation, GLSL shader development, and generative art prompts."

🔗 **Full repo:** [merrypranxter/THE-LISTS](https://github.com/merrypranxter/THE-LISTS)

---

## Folder Structure

| Folder | Contents |
|--------|----------|
| `aesthetics/` | Visual style vocabularies — glitch, internet eras, digital aesthetics |
| `moods/` | Atmospheric and emotional vectors |
| `operations/` | Transformative processes — chaos ops, acoustic resonance, optical effects |
| `phenomena/` | Observable physical phenomena |
| `states/` | Cognitive and perceptual states |
| `structures/` | Physical, mathematical, and biological structures |
| `systems/` | Dynamic systems — flow, reaction-diffusion, self-organization |
| `math-taxonomy-files/` | Extended math taxonomy (JSON + Markdown pairs) |

---

## Designed To Be Consumed By

- GLSL shader apps
- AI art prompt generators
- Generative art tools
- Vibecoded apps

---

## Usage Pattern

Pull a list, combine with a style, inject into a shader or prompt. These are vocabularies — building blocks for describing visual behavior, not final images.
