# 🎨 Fantastic Planet

> **One-sentence summary:** A 1970s French / Euro surreal science-fiction illustration style defined by dry ink linework, muted matte color, alien-biological absurdity, flattened composition, and a detached satirical mood.

🔗 **Full repo:** [merrypranxter/fantastic_planet_style](https://github.com/merrypranxter/fantastic_planet_style)

---

## Key Words

Roland Topor · La Planète Sauvage · Fantastic Planet · 1970s French sci-fi illustration · Euro surrealism · biological surrealism · alien ecology · satirical grotesque · dry ink drawing

---

## Fast Recognition Cues

- Hand-drawn fine contour lines
- Printed-paper texture
- Surreal flora/fauna (part insect, part organ, part botanical specimen)
- Strange scale relationships
- Matte dusty book-illustration palette
- Flattened / stage-like space
- Unsettling but not aggressive dream logic

---

## Core Identity

The defining magic is the collision of:

1. **Surrealism**
2. **Biological invention**
3. **Print-era illustration texture**
4. **Dry dark humor**
5. **Science-fiction worldbuilding without blockbuster spectacle**

---

## Emotional Tone

Dreamlike · clinical · strange · mildly cruel · detached · poetic in a dry way · fascinated by lifeforms without sentimentalizing them
