# 🎨 New Age Glam Fantasy

> **One-sentence summary:** New Age Glam Fantasy is retro mystical glamour with cosmic symbolism, soft-focus bloom, prismatic pastels, and analog decay.

🔗 **Full repo:** [merrypranxter/merry-style](https://github.com/merrypranxter/merry-style)

---

## Key Words

Mystical. Glamorous. Cosmic. Analog. Prismatic.

---

## Style Pillars

1. **Mystical metaphysical imagery** — crystals, portals, moons, pyramids, temples, auras, veils, divine light, psychic symbolism
2. **Glamour language** — gloss, satin, metallics, styled hair, dramatic makeup, jewelry, elegant poses, cinematic beauty lighting
3. **Soft optical diffusion** — halation, bloom, lens haze, pearlescent highlights, glowing edges, dreamlike softness
4. **Prismatic color** — pink, lavender, cyan, opal, ice blue, peach, pearl white, gold, spectral rainbow flare
5. **Analog wear** — tape softness, color drift, scanlines, chroma separation, print fade, mild distortion, generational loss
6. **Cosmic environments** — star fields, cloud chambers, celestial desert horizons, reflective black water, glowing interiors, mirrored voids
7. **Emotional transcendence** — initiation, enchantment, spiritual glamour, destiny, romance, visionary revelation, sacred drama

---

## Quick Visual Checklist

- [ ] Mystical/metaphysical subject or setting (crystals, portals, celestial bodies, sacred geometry)
- [ ] Glamour elements present (styled hair, makeup, jewelry, elegant clothing, beauty lighting)
- [ ] Soft optical diffusion (bloom, halation, lens haze, glowing edges)
- [ ] Prismatic pastel palette (pink, lavender, cyan, peach, pearl white, gold)
- [ ] Analog texture or degradation (film grain, tape softness, color drift, scanlines)
- [ ] Cosmic or otherworldly environment (star fields, reflective water, glowing interior)
- [ ] Emotional register: transcendent, enchanted, initiated, destined

---

## What This Style Is NOT

- not generic fantasy art
- not dark occult horror
- not cyberpunk neon
- not synthwave outrun
- not vaporwave collage
- not clean contemporary luxury surrealism
- not Y2K chrome futurism
- not sterile AI "ethereal woman in space" imagery
